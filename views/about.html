About StudyRacer


StudyRacer is created by a group of IA students at Malmö University during the spring semester 2022. The goal of the site is to provide students of all levels a better and more fun learning experience.

Whether you are a university student or in high school, you can benefit of our learning service StudyRacer. This is because it is scientifically proven (Källa) that it is easier for our brain to remember and learn if we use more than one cognitive ability at a time and by using StudyRacer you will be reading and writing at the same time. But not only does StudyRacer provide you with a learning tool to help you remember and learn texts easier you will also get better at speed writing, a useful skill in today’s society.
